# Appendix E. Glossary

- **AMT:** Address Morphism Theory.
- **Referent:** The entity an address expression points to.
- **Surface expression:** Human or machine visible address input.
- **Candidate set:** Possible referents generated from evidence.
- **Abstention:** A resolver's safe refusal to issue a precise identifier.
- **PID:** Persistent identifier issued after safe resolution.
- **View:** Purpose-specific representation derived from a referent or PID.
- **Address transaction:** A purpose-bound authorized use of an address referent.
- **Address authorization:** A decision that an actor may use an address token for a declared purpose.
- **Address capture:** A claim that an authorized address use was consumed by an operation.
- **Address settlement:** Reconciliation of receipts and policy decisions after address use.
- **Address reversal:** Cancellation or successor replacement before final settlement.

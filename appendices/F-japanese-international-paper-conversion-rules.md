# Appendix F. Japanese International Paper Conversion Rules

- Keep Japanese concepts when they carry institutional meaning.
- Translate claims into short English propositions before expanding prose.
- Avoid claiming worldwide empirical validation unless a verification file
  supports it.
- Use `address reference`, `referent`, `morphism`, `candidate sufficiency`,
  and `safe issuance` consistently.
- Keep examples synthetic unless a source and license are declared.

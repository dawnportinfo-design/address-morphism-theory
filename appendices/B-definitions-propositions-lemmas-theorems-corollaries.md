# Appendix B. Definitions, Propositions, Lemmas, Theorems, Corollaries

## Definitions

1. Surface address expression.
2. Registrable entity.
3. Candidate sufficiency.
4. Evidence-bound referent.

## Propositions

- Normalization does not imply referent equality.
- Candidate insufficiency requires abstention.
- Purpose-specific views can preserve a PID without exposing the full referent.

## Lemmas

- Entropy reduction is not equivalent to privacy safety.
- History-preserving replacement is distinguishable from destructive overwrite.

## Theorems

- Safe issuance theorem.
- Abstention monotonicity theorem.
- Context-relative optimality theorem.

## Corollaries

- Postal-code absence does not imply address impossibility.
- Coordinates alone do not imply delivery sufficiency.

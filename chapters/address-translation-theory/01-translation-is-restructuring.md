# Translation Is Restructuring

Address translation is not ordinary sentence translation. A good translation
preserves address structure, institutional meaning, and operational usability.

The correct output depends on purpose:

- international shipping label
- domestic delivery
- ecommerce form
- identity verification
- map search
- privacy-preserving proof

Therefore the translation target is not just a language. It is a language,
country model, institutional form, and use case.

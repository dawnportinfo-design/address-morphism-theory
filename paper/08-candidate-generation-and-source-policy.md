# 8. Candidate Generation And Source Policy

Candidate generation must be source-aware. Official data, postal data, open
geography, local registries, OSM/Overture-like sources, field reports, and
institutional records have different authority and failure modes.

AMT does not assume global completeness. It records candidate sufficiency as an
explicit condition and keeps source policy separate from the morphism itself.

Model hook: `formal/verification-map.ts`.

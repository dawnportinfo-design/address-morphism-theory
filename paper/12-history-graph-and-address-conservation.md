# 12. History Graph And Address Conservation

Address systems change. Streets are renamed, towns merge, buildings are split,
postal codes are revised, and political or institutional boundaries shift.

AMT models these changes as a history graph. A conservation principle states
that identity-changing operations must leave enough lineage to explain what was
preserved, split, merged, deprecated, or replaced.

Model hook: `formal/history-graph.ts`, `diagrams/history-graph.mmd`.

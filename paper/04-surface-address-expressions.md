# 4. Surface Address Expressions

A surface address expression is the visible or transmitted form of an address:
text, postal code, alias, QR, coordinate, building name, form fields, or a
human-readable label.

Surface expressions are not automatically truth. They are inputs to a resolver.
They can be incomplete, stale, ambiguous, translated, misspelled, culturally
specific, or intentionally privacy-limited.

AMT therefore distinguishes expression normalization from referent resolution.

Model hook: `formal/morphism-chain.ts`.
